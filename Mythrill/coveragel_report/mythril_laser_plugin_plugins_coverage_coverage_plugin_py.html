20class InstructionCoveragePlugin(LaserPlugin): 

21 """InstructionCoveragePlugin 

22 

23 This plugin measures the instruction coverage of mythril. 

24 The instruction coverage is the ratio between the instructions that have been executed 

25 and the total amount of instructions.

28 reachability will not be considered for the calculation of instruction coverage.
